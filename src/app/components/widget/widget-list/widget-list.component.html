What Will God’s Kingdom Accomplish?

The Bible’s answer

Once that happens, God’s Kingdom will . . . Remove the wicked, whose selfishness harms us all. “As regards the wicked, they will be cut off from the very earth.”—Proverbs 2:22.
End all wars. “[God] is making wars to cease to the extremity of the earth.”—Psalm 46:9.
